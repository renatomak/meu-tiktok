# REFERENCE AND FIDELITY PRIORITY

Use only the original reference images to reproduce the exact same real product. Do not create a similar, inspired, redesigned, beautified, standardized, stylized, or modernized version. The highest priority is visual fidelity to the original handcrafted piece, including its structure, proportions, irregularities, imperfections, colors, bead sequence, and central firma.

## 1. MAIN PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Generate a new ultra-realistic photograph of one single long handcrafted beaded guide necklace.

The necklace must be one continuous closed loop:

- no clasp;
- no hook;
- no opening;
- no loose ends;
- no visible connection hardware;
- no separate or unfinished extremities.

The entire strand must be made of small handcrafted beads in black and red.

The mandatory color sequence is:

3 black beads followed by 3 red beads.

Repeat this exact sequence continuously throughout the entire necklace:

3 black + 3 red + 3 black + 3 red.

Do not shuffle, randomize, reverse, interrupt, or replace this pattern.

The beads must retain the clearly handmade appearance visible in the reference images. They must have noticeably irregular and discrepant sizes, heights, widths, volumes, and shapes. Some beads may be slightly wider, narrower, taller, shorter, rounder, squarer, or more uneven than the neighboring beads.

These differences must look natural and handcrafted, not like defects added artificially. The beads must not appear mechanically identical, perfectly symmetrical, industrially manufactured, or excessively refined.

The necklace must contain one single larger central handcrafted bead, known as the firma. The firma must remain integrated into the continuous strand and proportional to the smaller beads.

The firma must be:

- elongated;
- dark and glossy;
- predominantly black;
- decorated with irregular red streaks, bands, swirls, or marbled details;
- handmade and slightly irregular in appearance;
- visually faithful to the central firma shown in the original reference images.

Preserve the exact identity of the product: one long, continuous, closed handcrafted guide necklace, made from irregular black and red beads in a repeated 3-black-and-3-red sequence, with one black-and-red central firma.

## 2. PRODUCT DISPLAY SCENARIO

Show the entire necklace clearly inside the frame, arranged naturally so that its continuous closed structure can be understood.

Display it on a simple, light, neutral background that does not compete with the product.

Use:

- soft natural lighting;
- balanced exposure;
- controlled reflections on the glossy beads;
- realistic and delicate shadows;
- sharp focus;
- visible handcrafted texture;
- high photographic realism;
- a clean and uncluttered product composition.

The product must remain the dominant subject of the image. The setting must not hide, distort, crop, separate, or visually alter any important part of the necklace or its central firma.

## 3. NEGATIVE INSTRUCTIONS AND PROHIBITED CHANGES

Do not reinterpret, redesign, stylize, modernize, beautify, standardize, simplify, or make the product more sophisticated than the original reference.

Do not change:

- the structure of the necklace;
- the continuous closed-loop construction;
- the relative proportions;
- the handcrafted irregularity;
- the black-and-red colors;
- the repeated sequence of 3 black beads followed by 3 red beads;
- the number or appearance of the central firma.

Do not generate:

- random color arrangements;
- one-black-one-red alternation;
- groups containing a different number of beads;
- perfectly uniform beads;
- identical beads;
- perfectly round beads;
- perfectly cylindrical beads;
- disc-shaped beads;
- heishi beads;
- flat spacer beads;
- large gemstone-like beads;
- pearl-like beads;
- industrial-looking beads;
- excessively polished or luxury-jewelry finishes.

Do not add:

- a clasp;
- a hook;
- an opening;
- loose ends;
- unfinished tips;
- metal terminals;
- metal chains;
- metal separators;
- pendants;
- medals;
- crosses;
- tridents;
- letters;
- logos;
- religious symbols;
- packaging;
- additional decorative pieces;
- extra necklaces or bracelets.

Do not duplicate the necklace.

Do not detach, hide, replace, resize, redesign, or change the shape of the central firma.

Do not make the necklace appear broken, open, separated, unfinished, or assembled from disconnected sections.

## FINAL PRIORITY

The final image must look like a new photograph of the exact same physical product shown in the original reference images—not a similar product and not a creative interpretation.

If there is any conflict between visual beauty and product fidelity, always prioritize fidelity to the original product.
